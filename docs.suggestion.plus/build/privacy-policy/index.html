Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy​

Suggestion Plus is built around the core principle of anonymity. We believe honest feedback can only happen when people feel safe sharing their thoughts.

Information We Collect​

For Submitters (People leaving suggestions)​

  • No personal information — We do not collect names, email addresses, IP addresses, or any personally identifiable information
  • Suggestion content — The text and any optional metadata you provide with your suggestion
  • No tracking — We do not use tracking cookies or analytics on suggestion submission pages

For Inbox Owners​

  • Account information — Email address and display name when you sign up
  • Inbox data — The inboxes you create, suggestions you receive, and your notification preferences
  • Payment data — If you purchase Premium, payment is processed securely by third-party providers; we do not store payment details

How We Use Information​

  • To deliver suggestions to the inbox owner
  • To send notifications based on your preferences
  • To improve and maintain the platform
  • To provide customer support

Data Retention​

  • Suggestions are retained until the inbox owner deletes them
  • Account data is retained until you delete your account
  • We do not retain any data on submitters

Third-Party Services​

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication, database, and cloud functions
  • Stripe / Google Play / App Store — Payment processing for Premium subscriptions
  • Email service — For notification emails

Each third party has its own privacy policy governing data handling.

Your Rights​

  • Access — Request a copy of your account data
  • Deletion — Delete your account and associated data at any time
  • Opt-out — Disable notifications or close your inbox at any time

Contact​

For privacy questions, contact support@letsdosimple.com
