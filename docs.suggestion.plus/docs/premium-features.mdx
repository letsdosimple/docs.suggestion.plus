## Premium Features in Detail

### Custom Box Codes

Create branded, memorable links for your inboxes. Instead of a long URL, use your own custom code.

```
suggestion.plus/YourBrand
```

Perfect for businesses, events, and organizations.

### Unlimited Everything

No caps on the number of inboxes or suggestions you can create and receive.

### Priority Support

Get priority response times and dedicated support from our team.

### Ad-Free

Remove all advertisements from your dashboard and suggestion pages for a clean experience.

### Advanced Analytics

Access detailed analytics including:

- Submission trends over time
- Category breakdowns
- Engagement metrics
- Exportable reports

## How to Upgrade

1. Go to your [dashboard](https://suggestion.plus/dashboard)
2. Navigate to Settings → Subscription
3. Choose your plan
4. Complete payment

Available on web, Google Play, and the App Store.
