# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### What is Suggestion Plus?

Suggestion Plus is an anonymous suggestion box platform that helps you collect honest feedback from your team, classroom, or community without compromising their privacy.

### Is it really anonymous?

Yes. We do not log IP addresses, require email addresses, or retain any personal data. Submissions are completely anonymous.

### Do I need an account to submit a suggestion?

No. Anyone can submit a suggestion to any public inbox without signing up. Only inbox owners need an account to manage their inbox.

## Setup & Usage

### How do I create an inbox?

Visit [suggestion.plus](https://suggestion.plus), sign up, and click "Create New Inbox". Name it, add a description, and set your notification preferences. It takes under 60 seconds.

### Can I customize my inbox link?

Yes. Premium users can create custom box codes for branded sharing links.

### How do I share my inbox?

You get a unique QR code and shareable link. Print the QR code for physical locations or share the link via email, WhatsApp, social media, or meetings.

## Notifications

### What kind of notifications do I get?

You can receive push notifications in the dashboard, email notifications, or both whenever a new suggestion is submitted.

### Can I turn off notifications?

Yes. You can configure notification preferences per inbox from your dashboard settings.

## Mobile Apps

### Is there a mobile app?

Yes. Suggestion Plus is available on both Android and iOS.

- [Download on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.letsdosimple.suggestionplus)
- [Download on App Store](https://apps.apple.com/in/app/suggestion/id6754521682)

### Are the mobile apps free?

Yes, the mobile apps are free to download and use. Premium features are available via in-app purchase.

## Privacy & Security

### Do you store my data?

We do not store any personal information about submitters. Inbox owners' account data is stored securely using Firebase Authentication.

### Is my data encrypted?

All data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored securely using Firebase's encryption standards.

## Troubleshooting

### I'm not receiving notifications

Check your notification preferences in your inbox settings. Ensure email notifications are enabled and check your spam folder if using email.

### My QR code isn't working

Make sure you're using the latest QR code from your inbox settings. Try regenerating the QR code if the issue persists.
