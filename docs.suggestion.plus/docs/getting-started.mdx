# Getting Started with Suggestion Plus

Welcome to Suggestion Plus documentation! This guide will help you understand how to use Suggestion Plus for your team, classroom, or community.

## What is Suggestion Plus?

Suggestion Plus is an anonymous suggestion box platform that helps you collect honest feedback from your audience without compromising their privacy.

## Quick Start

### Step 1: Create Your Inbox

1. Visit [suggestion.plus](https://suggestion.plus)
2. Sign in or create a new account
3. Click "Create New Inbox"
4. Name your inbox and add a description
5. Set notification preferences
6. Done! Your inbox is ready in under 60 seconds

### Step 2: Share Your Inbox

Get your unique QR code and shareable link:

- **QR Code**: Print and display on walls, posters, or screens
- **Link**: Share via WhatsApp, email, social media, or meetings

### Step 3: Collect Suggestions

Suggestions will appear in your inbox categorized as:

- **New**: New suggestions you haven't seen
- **Viewed**: Suggestions you've reviewed
- **Favorites**: Pin important suggestions for later
